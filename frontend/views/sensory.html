Sensory Play Days On Ezer Farm

Training for homeschooling moms/dads to help your child with motor coordination, sensory processing, social and emotional learning, fine motor skills and more.

Sensory Play Days

This is for our homeschooling moms and anyone interested in learning more about teaching pre-writing, visual perceptual skills, fine motor skills, the role of the sensory system in learning and regulating emotions and behaviors, and its positive effect on attention, learning and executive functioning skills.

My Experience

I have been an Occupational Therapist for 26 years and I've taught many teachers and educators tools for multi-sensory learning and success for children for school skills. I used to own my own handwriting clinic, because I realized how much self-esteem building there was in a child's ability to express themselves without limitations.

Bi-Monthly Sessions

Beginning in May, I will be holding bi-monthly "Sensory Play Fridays" at the farm and incorporating the animals - we have alpacas, goats, bunnies and mini-horses! While the children have some free play time, I want to teach you, "their educators" strategies for handwriting, visual motor skills, and all the areas mentioned above. It all starts with the right habits, so you don't have to "fix" those later. Click here to schedule a session.

What to Expect

  • Important: Parents stay with their children.
  • Bring clothes and shoes that can get very messy or wet at times
  • Bring snacks and drinks for the kids, there will be picnic tables available
  • 2 hours of play on the farm playground, animal interactions for sensory, and sensory play activities
  • $15 per family for 2 hour slot (Mom/Dad plus all kids from the family)
